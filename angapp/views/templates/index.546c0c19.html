How It Works

We create a transparent and efficient market to drive innovation for consumer brands. We provide capital in the form of equity, debt, or both, based on the business's unique needs:

Create a project that to which other investors will inject funds

You may find a project to fund in our list and invest according to your capacity

When a project has reached it's targeted funds, the loan will be paid and Lloyd CrowdFunding will redistribute the funds to the lenders.

The Products

The promoter(s) can choose to invite the funds to come into the business as equity, quasi equity, a loan or a combination of the options

EQUITY

The recommended range of equity stake to be made available is between 10% and 45% of the funded company.

QUASI EQUITY

These include Preference shares, debentures etc, together with all the options that come with such instruments (convertible, redeemable, cumulative, participating etc)

LOAN

Vanilla loans will carry a market related risk driven interest rate, and will be for a minimum of 6 months and a maximum of 3 years.

EQUITY/LOAN COMBINATION

Combinations of the above in different proportions will be possible, depending on the project being financed.

The selected financing option(s) will be on the advice of LCC, and the financier, and will depend on a number of project related criteria.
