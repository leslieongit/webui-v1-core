The Promoters

Equity Crowdfunding, also referred to as Crowd-investing, Investment Crowdfunding, or Crowd Equity is the online offering of financial securities of a private company to a group of people for investment.
As such it constitutes part of the capital market. Equity crowdfunding is often subject to securities and financial regulation because it involves investment into a commercial enterprise.
Equity Crowdfunding presents a new regime for SME Financing in Africa, enabling broad groups of investors to fund start-up companies and small businesses in return for equity. Its potential is greatest with start-up businesses that are seeking smaller investments to achieve establishment, while follow-on funding (required for subsequent growth) may come from other sources, as well as fresh rounds of crowdfunding.If the business succeeds, then its value goes up, as well as the value of a share in that business—the converse is also true.
The crowdfunding system has generally been used for charity and personal fundraising efforts. However, a number of SMEs in Africa (e.g. Mauritius, South Africa, Morocco, Tunisia, Cote d’Ivoire, Senegal etc) have successfully managed to raise capital for business start-ups and growth through Equity Crowdfunding.
The simplified approach is that LCF will facilitate the coming together of investors and investees that would probably not have had a chance to come together any other way.

  • This facility affords businesses with the opportunity for growth by providing funding in situations that would not have made this possible through the standard sources of capital.
  • This solution attempts to make sure that the business owner gets charged a reasonable interest, whilst the depositors also get a fair return for their money, in line with the attendant risks.
  • An Equity Crowdfunding Campaign increases online visibility which is great for the business, potential partners, sponsors, etc.This can give the business a significant boost in its growth plan.
  • This is also an opportunity to market-test the brand/product/service and develop a new customer base as investors become loyal customers.
